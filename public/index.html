Good to know

Before you come in

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Both work. Walk-ins are welcome, but booking online holds your spot so you're not waiting behind a full house — especially in the evenings, when things get busiest.

Can I request a specific barber?

Yes — pick Jose or Imad in the booking widget, or choose "no preference" to get the first open chair.

Do you cut kids' hair?

Yes, regularly. The shop is set up to be comfortable for younger clients, including a little patience and something to watch while they sit.

What if I need to cancel or change my time?

Call the shop at (848) 235-5919 with your confirmation code and we'll move things around — no charge for a normal reschedule.

Is parking available?

Yes, there's a shared lot for the Inman Ave shopping center right out front.
